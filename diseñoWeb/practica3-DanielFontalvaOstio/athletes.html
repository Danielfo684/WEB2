Adam Ondra

Known as one of the most accomplished climbers in the world, Adam Ondra has been pushing the boundaries of climbing for years. Hailing from Czechia, Ondra has achieved numerous first ascents, including some of the hardest routes ever climbed, such as "Silence" (graded 9c/5.15d). With a career starting at an early age, Adam is celebrated for his unparalleled technique, strength, and determination.

Janja Garnbret

A six-time world champion, Janja Garnbret is widely regarded as the best female climber in history. Hailing from Slovenia, Janja became the first-ever Olympic champion in sport climbing in Tokyo 2020. Known for her dominance in both lead and bouldering competitions, she continues to inspire climbers with her incredible focus, skill, and positivity.

Alberto Ginés

Representing Spain, Alberto Ginés became a household name after winning the first Olympic gold medal in sport climbing at Tokyo 2020. Alberto is a pioneer for the sport in his country and is recognized for his versatility across all climbing disciplines. He is part of the rising generation of climbers making a mark on the international stage.

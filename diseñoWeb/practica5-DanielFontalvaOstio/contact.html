Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Türkiye?

Visa requirements vary depending on your nationality. Many countries are eligible for e-Visas, which can be easily obtained online. Check the official government website for detailed information.

What is the best time of year to visit Türkiye?

Türkiye offers something unique year-round. Spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) are ideal for pleasant weather and fewer crowds, especially for exploring historical sites and cities.

What currency is used in Türkiye?

The currency used in Türkiye is the Turkish Lira (TRY). Credit cards are widely accepted in most tourist areas, but it’s advisable to carry some cash for smaller purchases.

Is Türkiye safe for tourists?

Türkiye is generally a safe destination for tourists. As with any travel, it’s always good to stay informed about local conditions and follow general safety precautions.

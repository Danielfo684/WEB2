About the minister

The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Turkey is dedicated to promoting the country's interests abroad, fostering international cooperation, and ensuring the well-being of Turkish citizens worldwide. Through diplomacy, strategic partnerships, and active participation in international organizations, the ministry plays a pivotal role in shaping Turkey's foreign policy.

Functions of the Ministry

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Turkey has a wide range of responsibilities, including:

  • Negotiating treaties and agreements with other nations.
  • Protecting the rights and interests of Turkish citizens abroad.
  • Coordinating Turkey's participation in international organizations.
  • Promoting cultural and economic ties with other countries.
  • Responding to global challenges and contributing to international peace and stability.

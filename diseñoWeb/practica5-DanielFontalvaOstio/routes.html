3-Day Istanbul Itinerary

Day 1: Historical Istanbul

Morning: Explore Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, and Basilica Cistern.

Afternoon: Visit Topkapı Palace and enjoy a traditional Turkish lunch.

Evening: Stroll through the Grand Bazaar and enjoy dinner with a view.

Day 2: Bosphorus and Modern Istanbul

Morning: Take a Bosphorus Cruise and visit Dolmabahçe Palace.

Afternoon: Explore Taksim Square, Istiklal Avenue, and Galata Tower.

Evening: Enjoy Istanbul’s nightlife or traditional Turkish music in Karaköy.

Day 3: Cultural and Local Experiences

Morning: Visit Spice Bazaar and Süleymaniye Mosque.

Afternoon: Explore Kadıköy on the Asian side and enjoy local cuisine.

Evening: Relax in Arnavutköy and watch the sunset by the Bosphorus Bridge.

Cappadocia

The Land of Fairy Chimneys

3-Day Cappadocia Itinerary

Day 1: Discovering the Unique Landscape

Morning: Start your day with a magical hot air balloon ride over the fairy chimneys.

Afternoon: Visit the Göreme Open-Air Museum, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and explore ancient rock-hewn churches.

Evening: Walk through the enchanting Pasabag (Monks Valley) and enjoy a quiet dinner in Göreme village.

Day 2: History and Adventure

Morning: Explore Derinkuyu or Kaymaklı Underground City, marveling at ancient subterranean living spaces.

Afternoon: Hike through the Ihlara Valley, a lush canyon with hidden churches and a peaceful river.

Evening: Relax in a traditional cave hotel and enjoy a meal of regional specialties like pottery kebabs.

Day 3: Culture and Natural Wonders

Morning: Visit Uchisar Castle, the highest point in Cappadocia, offering panoramic views of the region.

Afternoon: Discover the Devrent Valley (Imagination Valley), known for its unique rock formations resembling animals.

Evening: End your trip with a stunning sunset view from Red Valley and a traditional Whirling Dervish ceremony.

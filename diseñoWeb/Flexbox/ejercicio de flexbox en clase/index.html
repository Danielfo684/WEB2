Artic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys is an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. Known for their sharp lyrics, distinctive guitar sound, and charismatic stage presence, they quickly rose to fame with their debut album Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not (2006) , which became the fastest-selling debut album in UK chart history.
